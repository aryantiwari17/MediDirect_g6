Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about visiting or being treated at our hospital.

How do I schedule an appointment with a specialist?

You can schedule an appointment in several ways: through our online patient portal, by calling our appointment desk at +91 124 4141414, or by visiting the hospital in person. For new patients, we recommend arriving 30 minutes before your scheduled appointment time to complete registration paperwork.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring your photo ID, insurance card (if applicable), any relevant medical records or test results from other providers, a list of current medications, and a method of payment. For first-time patients, you may download and complete our patient forms in advance from our website.

What are the visiting hours for patients?

General visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily. ICU visiting hours are more restricted, typically limited to two 30-minute periods per day. Pediatrics allows parents to stay overnight. Please check with the nursing station for department-specific visiting policies, as some areas may have additional restrictions.

Do you accept my health insurance?

MediDirect - The Medicity has agreements with most major insurance providers in India and several international insurers. We recommend contacting your insurance company to verify coverage before your visit. Our financial counselors can also assist with insurance questions and pre-authorizations when needed.

Where can visitors park and are there any charges?

We have ample parking available on campus. The first 30 minutes are free, with nominal charges for longer stays. Valet parking is available at the main entrance for a small fee. Patients being admitted or discharged may qualify for free parking - please ask at the reception desk.

How can I access my medical records?

You can access portions of your medical records through our secure patient portal. For complete records, you may submit a request at the Medical Records department with proper identification. There may be nominal fees for copying records. Turnaround time is typically 3-5 business days for standard requests.

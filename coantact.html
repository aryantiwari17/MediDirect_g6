Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting MediDirect and our services.

How do I book an appointment at MediDirect?

You can book an appointment through multiple channels:

  • Call our appointment helpline at +91 124 4141414
  • Use our online appointment system on the website
  • Visit the hospital in person at our reception
  • Email us at appointments@MediDirect.org

What are the visiting hours for patients?

General visiting hours are from 11:00 AM to 12:30 PM and 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM daily. However, ICU visiting hours may vary and are limited to immediate family members. Please check with the nursing station for specific ICU visiting policies.

Does MediDirect accept international insurance?

Yes, MediDirect has tie-ups with several international insurance providers. Our International Patient Services team can assist with insurance verification and processing. Please contact our International Patient Desk at +91 124 4141500 or email international@MediDirect.org for assistance.

How can I get my medical records from MediDirect?

Medical records can be obtained by submitting a request at the Medical Records Department or through our online portal. You will need to provide valid identification and complete the required authorization forms. There may be nominal charges for processing medical records requests.

What COVID-19 safety measures are in place at MediDirect?

MediDirect follows all recommended COVID-19 safety protocols including:

  • Mandatory temperature checks at all entry points
  • Hand sanitization stations throughout the facility
  • Regular disinfection of all areas
  • Social distancing measures in waiting areas
  • PPE for all healthcare staff
  • Separate pathways for suspected COVID-19 cases

Please wear a mask at all times while in the hospital premises.

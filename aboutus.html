Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming India's most trusted multi-specialty hospital

Transforming Healthcare in India

Founded in 2009 by renowned cardiac surgeon Dr. Naresh Trehan, MediDirect - The Medicity was conceived as an institution that would bring together the brightest medical minds to provide world-class healthcare in India. What began as a vision to create a center of excellence has now grown into one of India's largest and most respected multi-specialty hospitals.

Spread across 43 acres in Gurugram, MediDirect houses over 1,250 beds, 45 operating theaters, and state-of-the-art facilities for over 62 medical specialties. Our institution is built on the pillars of clinical excellence, patient-centric care, and cutting-edge research.

As the first hospital in India to be accredited by Joint Commission International (JCI), we maintain the highest global standards in healthcare delivery. Our team of 400+ super-specialists, many trained at premier international institutions, work collaboratively to provide comprehensive, integrated care.
